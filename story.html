Planning, Brainstorming, and Having Fun!

Welcome to the world of Sutherland Manor!

Narrowing our options...

We went through a protracted brainstorming process to dream up a project for the digital space. We were thinking of renovating a classic (like the "Song of Roland") and making it more exciting with interactive components and engaging illustrations and animations. However, we finally hit upon what we think is a great idea: an interactive mystery project (a la Agatha Christie...but in cyberspace)!
